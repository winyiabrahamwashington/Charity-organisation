About Us

Chicken Chicken Charity is a newly established organization with a simple yet powerful vision

Why We Exist

In Uganda, millions struggle with poverty, lack of access to education, and inadequate healthcare. While large-scale aid programs exist, we believe in the power of focused, personal transformation.

Our name "Chicken Chicken" reflects our Ugandan roots and our commitment to approachable, grassroots change. Just as small steps lead to big journeys, helping one person at a time creates ripple effects that transform communities.

Founded in 2023, we're starting small but dreaming big. Our goal is simple: each year, we will completely transform the life of one deserving Ugandan individual through comprehensive support.

Our Mission

Our Mission

We're committed to creating lasting change through focused, individual support

Education

We provide full scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship to ensure our selected individual receives quality education.

Healthcare

Comprehensive medical coverage including check-ups, treatments, and health education for our beneficiaries.

Family Support

We extend our help to the immediate family of our beneficiary, providing basic necessities and livelihood support.

Sustainable Change

Our support continues until the individual is fully empowered to sustain themselves and contribute to their community.
